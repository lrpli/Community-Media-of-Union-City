The People Behind the Mission

Our Team

Community Media of Union City is powered by dedicated staff, committed volunteers, and a board of directors who give their time and talent to keep local media alive and thriving.

Leadership

Executive Director

Jason McCray

Executive Director & Principal Officer

Jason McCray has been a driving force behind Community Media of Union City's growth and impact. As Executive Director and Principal Officer, Jason leads the organization's strategic vision, community partnerships, and day-to-day operations — ensuring that CMUC remains true to its founding mission of amplifying local voices across northwestern Pennsylvania.

Jason's commitment to community media is rooted in a deep belief that every resident of Union City deserves access to the tools, training, and platforms that empower them to tell their own stories. Under his leadership, CMUC has expanded its programs, deepened its partnerships with schools and civic organizations, and grown its reach across the region.

Staff

Our Team Members

The talented individuals who make our programs run every day.

Maria Reyes

Program Coordinator

Maria oversees day-to-day program operations, volunteer scheduling, and participant onboarding across all four of CMUC's core programs. She brings a background in nonprofit administration and a passion for accessible education.

Derek Thompson

Media Production Trainer

Derek leads technical training for the Youth Media Lab and Public Access Television program. A veteran of local broadcast media, he brings professional-grade instruction and contagious enthusiasm for the craft of visual storytelling.

Aisha Lewis

Media Literacy Educator

Aisha designs and delivers CMUC's media literacy curriculum for schools and community groups across Erie County. With a background in education and communications, she makes complex media concepts accessible for learners of all ages and backgrounds.
